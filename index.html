Sign up by providing your email, creating a password, and entering other personal information. Verifying your email address and submitting documents for identity verification (KYC - Know Your Customer). Common documents include a government-issued ID and proof of address.
Deposit Funds
Log into your verified account. Navigate to the deposit section of the site. Choose the method you want to use, follow the instructions provided, and complete the deposit process.
Start Trading
